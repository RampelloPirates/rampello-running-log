Add to home screen

Install first, then sign in from the app — that keeps you signed in for good.

iPhone or iPad (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up (bottom of the screen on iPhone, top-right on iPad).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top right.
  4. Open the new icon, enter your email, and type the 6-digit code from the email.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the menu button (⋮) in the top right.
  2. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  3. Tap Install, then open the icon and sign in.

On iPhone, always use the 6-digit code inside the app. (Tapping the email link opens Safari instead of your installed app, so the app can stay signed out.)
